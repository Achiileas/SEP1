Cloverville Life Philosophies

  • Live Simply

    Small sustainable choices create a big impact.

  • Share Generously

    We exchange food, tools, skills, and support—community thrives through sharing.

  • Care for the Earth

    Every action should respect nature and help it flourish.

  • Celebrate Effort

    All green actions matter, big or small. Progress counts more than perfection.

  • Value Fairness

    Points help keep duties balanced—not competitive.

  • Learn Together

    We grow through shared knowledge, curiosity, and inspiration.

  • Keep It Joyful

    Sustainability is a way of living with creativity, intention, and joy.
