Welcome to Cloverville

Cloverville is a small, green village where 42 residents live sustainably and share everything—from vegetables and tools to stories and support. Life here is all about collaboration, creativity, and caring for the planet.

At the heart of the village is Green Bob, the eco-philosopher and coordinator, who keeps everyone inspired without turning sustainability into a competition. Villagers contribute in three ways:

  • Green Actions: Personal eco-friendly efforts like cycling, composting, or trying vegan meals. These add to the village’s shared green points.
  • Communal Tasks: Shared responsibilities such as cooking, gardening, or tidying communal spaces. Residents earn personal points for these contributions.
  • Trade Offers: Villagers exchange goods or services using personal points—from homegrown carrots to mending clothes.

Cloverville is not just about living sustainably—it is about inspiring each other and the wider world. Through green actions, communal efforts, and generosity, the villagers show how a small community can make a big difference for people and planet alike.
